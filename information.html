About Zynther Tiers

Zynther Tiers is the definitive ranking system designed for the competitive Minecraft PvP community. We track performance across various game modes to provide accurate, real-time standings.

How it Works

Players are ranked based on their total points, which are calculated through our custom API integration. Rankings are updated automatically as you play on our servers.

Tier System

We use a tiered classification system (HT1 - HT5 for High Tiers, LT1 - LT5 for Low Tiers) to categorize player skill levels across different disciplines like UHC, Pot, and NethOP.
